Before You Reach Out

Common questions

How long does a typical engagement last?

Individual coaching typically runs 6–12 months, with sessions 2–4 times per month. Some clients continue beyond that. Team engagements are typically structured as ongoing partnerships, with intensive offsite sessions held quarterly or bi-annually.

Do you work with clients outside of New York?

Yes, most of my clients work with me virtually over Zoom. I work with clients across North America, Europe, and beyond. Timezone flexibility is available.

How do I know if coaching is right for me?

If you're curious, open, and willing to do some honest inner work, coaching is probably right for you. The discovery call is designed to help both of us answer that question together.

What does a session actually look like?

Sessions are 60 minutes and follow your agenda. We might work through a specific challenge, explore a pattern you've noticed, or use a structured exercise. Every session ends with a clear next step.
